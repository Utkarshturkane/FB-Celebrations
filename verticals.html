Edulate
Launched: June 2020

Creating a spark in the minds of budding event planners, Edulate is an online specialized course offering students the opportunity to harness the knowledge of the events industry through structured sessions. It is a platform to make the potential talent of the event industry ready for a promising future. Helming Edulate, co-founders Farid Khan and Bhavnesh Sawhney are elated to associate with expert Event Planner and Trainer Neha Rathi, in event education.

Neha has mentored over 3000 students pan India over the last 4 years and has played a crucial part in curating and teaching the Wedding Planning course at NAEMD and EMDI. With a combination of rich expertise and experience, the course work will masterfully be designed to strike a balance of both, theoretical and practical aspects of the event planning business. Edulate will introduce a spectrum of new opportunities and learnings to the young and thriving minds of millennials.

FB Care
Launched: June 2020

FB Celebrations understands the uncertainty of the current times and the need to get back to a clean and healthy working environment.

It brings to you a new venture FB CARE that collaborates with the best international high tech companies that manufacture products to deal with biological outbreaks.

FB Expressions
Launched : June 2020

FB Expressions is a tool to customize creative content for brands to virtually Express More and Express Better. It is a creative studio that intends to effectively express and bring out the best in every brand. Tapping into the innate expertise of Farid Khan and Bhavnesh Sawhney, the creative studio will largely focus on Event and Communication Consultation, Content Creation and Graphic Solutions. Through this, FB Expressions will ensure credible communication that is best suited to your company and its events, customize creative content that digitally delves into your target audience, and design bespoke graphics that virtually voice a thousand thoughts, bringing worthwhile value to every brand. It is a vertical that will cater to all the digital desires of our clients.

FB Expressions is an initiative that will articulate every brand's story with a trendy twist. With a team of talented in-house maestros, we intend to introduce a myriad of exciting and disruptive trends in the industry and emerge as torchbearers in the event planning space.
