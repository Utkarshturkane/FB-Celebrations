Gaurav
& Pranjli’s

WEDDING CELEBRATION

Jaipur, Rajasthan

A well-planned three-day affair in Jaipur’s JW Marriott & The Fairmont Hotel was a zingy start to Gaurav & Pranjli’s love story, where over 900 guests enjoyed the perfect blend of traditional as well as modern themed events, like a nostalgic dinner party which was a night picked right out of your childhood, the Maha-Aarti to start off the celebrations, a carnival themed Mehendi & finally a Regal wedding.

Grand highlights were performances by the family members, Sukhbir, the ever so dazzling Neeti Mohan & DJ Amaan Nagpal. Some of the peaking elements were the hydraulics Ganesha statue unveiling to the guests at the end of the Maha aarti, the maroon drapes Jyothi deeps and bells, inspiration from royal temples, made it all look so divine. The fusion of the Rambo Indian circus & the European Atelier Carnival with interactive stalls and games, décor was multi coloured, bright & splashy.
